Privacy Policy

What data MapleSavings collects, how your calculator inputs are handled, and what third parties are involved.

Short version: Your income and contribution numbers are never sent to a server. All calculations happen in your browser. We do not collect personal information. No account, no cookies, no tracking pixels.

What This Policy Covers

This privacy policy describes how MapleSavings.ca ("MapleSavings", "we", "our") handles information when you use this website. If you have questions about anything in this policy, please contact us at contact.maplesavings@gmail.com.

Calculator Inputs — No Data Sent

When you use the MapleSavings tax calculator, the values you enter (your income, RRSP contribution room, FHSA contribution room, province, and tax year) are used exclusively by JavaScript running in your browser. These values:

  • Are never transmitted to any server — there is no back end processing your inputs
  • Are not stored in cookies or browser local storage
  • Are not logged or associated with your session
  • Are not shared with any third party, including Wealthsimple

The moment you close or navigate away from the page, all inputs are discarded.

Analytics

MapleSavings does not currently use any analytics service (such as Google Analytics). If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy. Any analytics we use will be aggregate-only (e.g., total page views, most popular province selected) and will not allow us to identify individual users or their inputs.

Cookies

MapleSavings does not set any cookies. No consent banner is required because no cookies are used.

Third Parties

Fonts

This site uses the Inter typeface, served directly from our own domain (self-hosted). No font requests are sent to Google or any other third party, and no data about you is shared through font loading.

Wealthsimple

MapleSavings has a referral relationship with Wealthsimple (see our Disclosure page). If you click a referral link and visit Wealthsimple, Wealthsimple's privacy policy applies to your interaction with their site. MapleSavings does not pass any of your calculator inputs to Wealthsimple. The only information shared via the referral link is a referral code embedded in the URL.

Contact and Email

If you contact us by email (contact.maplesavings@gmail.com), we will receive and retain the contents of that email for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. We do not add you to any list, share your email with third parties, or use it for marketing.

Children

MapleSavings is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Your Rights (PIPEDA / CASL)

Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) gives you rights over your personal information. Because we collect no personal information through normal use of this site, there is typically nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have contacted us by email and wish to have that correspondence deleted, please email us and we will confirm deletion.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date below. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
